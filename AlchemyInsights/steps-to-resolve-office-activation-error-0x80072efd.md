# <a name="steps-to-resolve-office-activation-error-0x80072efd"></a>Măsuri pentru a rezolva Office de activare de eroare 0x80072EFD

Incerca aceste solutii pentru a rezolva eroarea de activare Office:
  
1. Verifica [firewall-ul](https://support.office.com/article/0d23d3c0-c19c-4b2f-9845-5344fedc4380#BKMK_CheckFirewall).

2. A verifica al tău [antivirus produse de plastic](https://support.office.com/article/0d23d3c0-c19c-4b2f-9845-5344fedc4380#BKMK_CheckAV).

3. Verificaţi [setările proxy](https://support.office.com/article/0d23d3c0-c19c-4b2f-9845-5344fedc4380#BKMK_CheckProxy)

4. Descărca şi instala [sprijin si asistent de recuperare](https://aka.ms/SARA-OfficeActivation-Alchemy) şi executaţi **Depanatorul de activare**.
