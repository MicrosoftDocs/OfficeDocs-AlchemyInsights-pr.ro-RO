<span data-ttu-id="e62c2-p101">Începând cu 23 martie 2018, utilizatorii externi vor nu mai putea accesa conţinut care a fost repartizat cu toată lumea, toţi utilizatorii autentificaţi sau toţi utilizatorii de forme, chiar dacă acestea au fost invitat sau adaugă grupuri autorizat. Dacă doriţi să păstraţi setările existente, vă rugăm să consultaţi:https://support.microsoft.com/help/4089534/how-to-grant-the-everyone-claim-to-external-users-in-office-365</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e62c2-p101">Starting March 23, 2018, external users will no longer be able to access content that has been Shared with Everyone, All Authenticated Users, or All Forms Users, even if they have been invited or added to authorized groups. If you would like to keep your existing settings, please see: https://support.microsoft.com/help/4089534/how-to-grant-the-everyone-claim-to-external-users-in-office-365</span></span>
