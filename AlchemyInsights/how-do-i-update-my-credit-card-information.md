1. <span data-ttu-id="3fdd7-103">În Centrul de administrare Microsoft 365, accesați pagina **Facturare** \> [Metode de plată](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=842054).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3fdd7-103">In the Microsoft 365 admin center, go to the **Billing** \> [Payment methods](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=842054) page.</span></span>
